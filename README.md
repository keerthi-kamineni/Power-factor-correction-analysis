# Power-factor-correction-analysis
Power factor correction analysis using MATLAB with current, loss and visualization studies 
This repository documents a structured study of power factor correction and its impact on electrical systems.
Till now 5th march 2026 completed work 
1. Power fundamentals
2. Phase angle and power factor
3. Reactive power calculation
4. Capacitor required to correct the power factor
5. Converting capacitance KVAR to the microfarad
6. Impact of powerfactor on line current
7. Impact of powerfactor correction on line current and on the copper losses
8. Impact of powerfactor correction on line current graph
9. Impact of pf on cu loss graph
